--> Natural Language Processing (NLP) enables computers to understand and interpret human language

--> NLP bridges this gap by allowing machines to process and understand human languages, making it an essential tool in modern AI systems

################## Need for Natural Language Processing
**** By training NLP models, we can equip computers to process language data in a variety of forms, from written text to voice input
***  NLP breaks down language understanding into smaller, manageable components. This approach, known as the NLP pipeline, 
     involves several stages that collectively enable machines to interpret human language effective



################# Key Steps in Natural Language Processing (NLP) Pipeline
1. sentence segmentation  ::  breaking text into individual sentence

2. tokenization      ::  split sentence into tokens (words/punctuations)

3. predict parts of speech (POS) :: Assign POS tag for each tokens

4. Lemmatization      :: convert words to their root form

5. Stop word removal  :: Remove common words like 'a','and' , 'the'.........etc 

6. Dependency Parsing :: Create synthatic tree and understand word relationship

7. Named entry recognisation (NER) :: Identify entities (people , place , dates)

8. Coreference resolution :: resolve pronoun and expression referring to the smae entity
